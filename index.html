Northwest Notary

Serving Lane County and surrounding areas.

Available 7 days a week including holidays.

Evening hours available by appointment.

Services Provided

  • Living wills and trusts
  • Power of attorney
  • Jurats
  • Acknowledgement
  • Certified copies
  • Other general Notary acts

Time and Fees

  • All signings, in accordance with Oregon law, are $10 per signing plus travel fee
  • For Cottage Grove & Creswell areas, travel fee is $20
  • For central Eugene & Springfield areas, travel fee is $35
  • Areas outside of those listed above require a quote to calculate travel fee
  • Please note: Travel fees are due within 24 hours of appointment date. If same day appointment, travel fee is due immediately.

Miscellaneous Fees

  • Signer refuses to sign - travel fee is still required
  • Signer does not have required government ID - travel fee is still required
  • Wait times - An additional $10 is charged for every 15 minutes after scheduled appointment time
